# E - Purchase 

A Purchase Request and Order Tracking System is an application designed to streamline the creation, submission, and tracking of purchase requests (PR) and purchase orders (PO). It ensures accurate details of requested items, quantities, amounts, and dates, and allows users to easily search, retrieve, and verify purchase requests and orders.

By centralizing purchase records, the system enhances financial control, minimizes errors, and facilitates efficient auditing, reporting, and procurement management.
